What We Do

We help CDIS students create portfolios that are more attractive to recruiters and potential employers through structured opportunities to build and showcase skills outside the classroom.

Resume & LinkedIn Workshops

Professional resume and LinkedIn profile enhancement workshops to make you stand out to recruiters.

GitHub & Website Development

Build professional GitHub profiles and personal websites that showcase your technical skills and projects.

Mentorship & Interviews

Participate in mentorship pairings, mock technical interviews, and recruiter panels with industry professionals.

End-of-semester showcase events connecting you with faculty, alumni, and industry professionals.
